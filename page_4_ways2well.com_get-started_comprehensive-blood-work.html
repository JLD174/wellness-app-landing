FAQs

What does the Ways2Well Membership include?

For $599 per year, you'll receive:

What kinds of consultations are available?

Your first consultation with a Ways2Well clinician is part of your membership. This ensures you have a clear picture of your baseline to make educated decisions moving forward. If you would like additional visits, you have the option of $49 for 20 minutes or $99 for 40 minutes.

How will I receive my results and clinical note?

Results will be available in the Ways2Well app within 5–7 days. The clinical note will be delivered through the app.

How long are my blood results valid for a review?

We can only interpret and analyze your bloodwork within three months because lab results reflect your health at a specific point in time. After three months, many factors, such as lifestyle changes, diet, medications, stress, or natural changes in your body, can cause your values to shift. Reviewing results older than this may no longer provide an accurate or reliable picture of your current health, and could lead to recommendations that don’t reflect your present needs. For this reason, we limit our clinical review window to three months to ensure our guidance is timely, safe, and relevant to your care.

Where is the membership available?

Membership is only available in covered/licensed states . We no longer offer "digital only" membership in states where Ways2Well is not licensed.

How do I access everything included in my membership?

All benefits—including lab booking, results, clinical notes, and supplements/Rx—are accessible through the Ways2Well app.

What is the cancellation policy?

You can cancel your membership any time before your next renewal date through your account settings or by contacting our support team.
Once you cancel, you'll still have access to your membership benefits until the end of your current billing period. However, please keep in mind that any unused bloodwork credits will expire at the end of your current cycle, and memberships are non-refundable once billing has processed. We're also unable to offer refunds for partially used memberships.

What if I want more than two bloodworks per year?

You are welcome to purchase additional bloodwork panels by ordering through your provider directly.

Do I get to choose which labs are run?

The Ways2Well panels are designed by our clinicians to provide comprehensive insights. Customization may be available in future bloodwork, depending on your results and health needs.

Can I speak to a provider before getting bloodwork?

We understand you may have questions before moving forward. However, access to our licensed providers is available after becoming a member.

What states does Ways2Well serve?

Our services are available in the following covered states: https://ways2well.com/covered-states

Will someone help me interpret my results?

Yes. Your first comprehensive blood panel comes with a one-on-one deep dive with a Ways2Well protocol-trained clinician. Every panel includes an easy-to-understand clinician report written by our medical team, also you’ll have access to ALLEN, our custom-trained AI healthcare assistant, available 24/7 in the Ways2Well app. ALLEN is designed to deep dive into your biomarkers and answer any questions you have — whether about hormone levels, inflammation markers, or overall metabolic health. He’s been trained on thousands of hours of clinical data and provider insights, making him incredibly comprehensive, fast, and accurate. ALLEN helps you interpret lab values, identify trends, and prepare for follow-up care, giving you immediate clarity and guidance every step of the way.

Is this covered by insurance?

No. The membership is cash-pay only, and we do not bill insurance. However, you can utilize your HSA/FSA card for the membership payment.

Do you accept outside labs from my doctor?

Unfortunately, we cannot accept external labs at this time.

Is Ways2Well available internationally?

The Ways2Well Membership is currently available only in the following continental US states: https://ways2well.com/covered-states

Who is ALLEN?

ALLEN is our custom-built AI healthcare assistant who lives inside the Ways2Well app. He's been trained on thousands of hours of clinical data and real patient cases to help you decode your lab results and understand what's happening in your body. ALLEN can suggest personalized protocols, recommend lifestyle changes, or even roast you a little if that's the motivation you need. Whether you want science, support, or sass—ALLEN's got it handled, 24/7.
